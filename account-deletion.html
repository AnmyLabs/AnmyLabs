Last updated: July 5, 2026

Request Cloud Data Deletion

To request deletion of your PayDay cloud data, contact Anmy Labs at anmydotlabs@gmail.com with the Google email address used in PayDay.

What We Delete

We will delete cloud data associated with your PayDay account from Firebase where applicable. This may include backed up salary, commitments, bills, house, rent, income, settings, and related cloud sync records connected to your signed-in Google account.

Local Device Data

Local data stored on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings.
